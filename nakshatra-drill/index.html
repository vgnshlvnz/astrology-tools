South Indian Rāśi Chart

Fixed-position reference layout · clockwise from Pisces.

In South Indian charts, sign positions never move — Pisces is always top-left, Aries top-second, and so on clockwise. Only planets and the Lagna marker shift between cells based on the chart being read. Numbers are zodiacal sign indices (1 = Aries); the saffron line shows each sign's ruling planet (Parashari rāśi-pati). Note: sign lord ≠ nakshatra lord — the Vimshottari sequence is a separate cycle.

How to read your own rāśi chart

Step 1 — Find your Lagna cell

Your birth chart will mark one cell as Lag, Asc, or simply L. That cell is your Lagna — the sign rising on the eastern horizon at the moment of your birth. Locate that same sign in the reference grid above.

Step 2 — Count your houses clockwise

In the South Indian system, houses are counted clockwise from the Lagna cell. The Lagna cell itself is House 1 (tanu bhāva). The next cell clockwise is House 2, the one after that House 3, and so on around the grid. The sign numbers never move; only your starting point changes.

Step 3 — Place your planets

Each planet in your chart is listed with its sign (rāśi). Find that sign in the reference grid — the planet sits in that cell. A cell can hold multiple planets. The sign lord shown in saffron is the rāśi-pati of that bhāva, not the planet occupying it.

Step 4 — Read house + sign together

Each cell tells you two things at once: which bhāva it is (its house number, counted from your Lagna) and which rāśi it is (its fixed sign). A planet in that cell is simultaneously in that house and in that sign — both layers apply when interpreting.

Worked example — 11 March 1992, 06:13 AM

Rohini · Latitude +3.7 · Longitude +101.41

Step 1 — Lagna

The Lag marker is in the Aquarius cell — left column, second row from the top (sign index 11). Aquarius is House 1.

Step 2 — House sequence

Counting clockwise from Aquarius: Pisces = H2, Aries = H3, Taurus = H4, Gemini = H5, Cancer = H6, Leo = H7, Virgo = H8, Libra = H9, Scorpio = H10, Sagittarius = H11, Capricorn = H12.

Step 4 — House + sign together

Moon is in Taurus (fixed earth, ruled by Venus) and in H4 (sukha bhāva — home, mother, inner life). Taurus steadies the Moon; H4 is its natural domain. The Rohini nakshatra deepens this — Moon is in its own nakshatra, at its strongest placement in the zodiac.
